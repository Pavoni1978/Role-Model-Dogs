About Us

We are a family run business with over 20 years experiance in dog training.

We specialise in obedience trainng for general obedience out and about and at home.

We also specialise in obedience competition training at all levels.

We run classes at all levels from Puppy to Advanced classes.

Dogs will be taught to sit and lay down on command.

Dogs taught to heel on the left side on and off the lead.

We are actually teaching the handler who then actually trains their own dog.

We strive to make your dog alot more pleasant to take out for walks and better behaviour at home.

Obedience training is fantastic mental stimulation for all dogs.

All our instructors are vetted.

Instructors are all Level 4 accredited at Worldwide Dog Training Academy.
